Callis Timer

It's a simple and easy to use timer app for all common functional fitness workouts: AMRAP (As many rounds as possible), For Time, and EMOM (Every minute on a minute). Just choose the workout type, set the duration and GO!

Resources

Calisthenics exercises

Calisthenics exercises can be performed anywhere and anytime, with the only requirement being that one should have a decent amount of space to work out in.

Workouts

The app is utils to the workouts: Amrap (many rounds/reps as possible) and E.M.O.M (every minute on the minute).

E.M.O.M

Emom is the abbreviation of every minute on the minute. My favorite kind of workout. It means that every minute you must start the movement. Sometimes there are alternating minutes, even and odd numbered minutes, on even numbered minutes you do one exercise and the odd numbered another, it is also possible to have 4 or 5 minutes rolling.

Amrap

It is always followed by a number, that number means how many minutes you must work. For example amrap7, then you do the circuit for the whole 7 minutes before having a break. If it is a competition you have to count the rounds or reps you do in these minutes to get final score.
